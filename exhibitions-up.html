CURRENT EXHIBITIONS


BIBLIOMANIA 2018*

From our open call for artist books of 'Bibliomania 2018', we have 13 books from 12 artists that will be presented and sale at the exhibition as listed (in random order):

Kittiphol Saragganonda's Choose Your Own Adventure
Pam Virada's Dialogues Between Memory Objects
Rob Kovitz's The Sweets of Home
Eu Jin Lim's Drawing The Blue Mansion
Ekkapon Panpat's Barracuda and Killer Whale
Theetat Thunkijjanukij's RRAAAR
Nina Wiesnagrotzki's Game Camera
Jonathan Johnson's Low Season
Naraphat Sakarthornsap's Ignorant Bond
Saksit Khunkitti's Fragmented Stillness
Yeonjeong's Suxury
KG Augenstern's Tentacles

The subject of these books varied from a reflection upon fictional realm to a more objective observation of the so-called 'reality'. For now, this project focuses on particular medium, and we try to expand the horizon of this entity to cover more than its physical and functional meanings. Beside from the books exhibition, our intention for workshops, talks and discussions is to observe the terrain surrounding book as an artistic medium, more or less.
