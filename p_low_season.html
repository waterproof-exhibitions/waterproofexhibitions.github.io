LOW SEASON

JONATHAN JOHNSON


Low Season is poetic narrative featuring color photographic images made during, and in relationship to, the act of walking through urban and rural landscapes. In contrast to some contemporary uses of photography associated with digital culture, social media and self-documentation; this project embraces slowness through its production (walking, using color film) and dissemination through the book or sequential form--one of our final refuges for experiencing photographs in a personal, slow and physically interactive way.
